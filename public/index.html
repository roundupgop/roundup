What to expect

Roundup for Republicans provides an easy way for supporters to engage with your campaign.

With their permission, every purchase they make will be "rounded up" to the nearest dollar and we'll donate the spare change to your campaign*

Customer engagement flow

  1. User signs up for Roundup for Republicans Users can activate with an email address or common social login provider like Google.
  2. User links their bank account via Plaid Roundup for Republicans will never know users' bank account credentials.
  3. User connects a payment method via Stripe Likewise, Roundup for Republicans will never know donors' payment information
  4. At the end of the month The customer's "roundup amount" is calculated based on transaction history pulled securely from Plaid. The customer is then charged the "spare change" through their connected payment method.

FAQs

How will this help my campaign?

We provide a unique way for donors to contribute to your campaign. In addition to unlocking new fundraising sources, Roundup for Republicans aims to make donating to your campaign habitual. Donors can contribute throughout the year, helping you smooth out the cyclic nature of campaign fundraising
